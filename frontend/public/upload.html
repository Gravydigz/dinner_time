How to Process Uploaded Recipes

  1. Upload files: Drag and drop recipe images or PDFs above - they are saved directly to the server
  2. Import from URL: Paste a recipe URL to extract the recipe directly from the webpage
  3. Process with AI: Click the "Process with AI" button on any uploaded file to send it to the AI for recipe extraction
  4. Review & Save: Extracted recipes appear in the "Pending Review" section for editing before saving
  5. After processing: Files are automatically moved to data/uploads/processed/
