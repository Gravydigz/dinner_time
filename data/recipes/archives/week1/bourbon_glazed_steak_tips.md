# Bourbon-Glazed Steak Tips

**Source:** Beef Loving Texans
**Author:** Jess Pryles

## Ingredients
- 3 lb Top Blade Roast, Flat Iron, or Sirloin
- 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup plus 2 tbsp honey
- 1/2 cup plus 2 tbsp bourbon
- Spray oil
- 1/2 tsp salt

## Instructions
1. Cube beef into 1-inch pieces. Remove and discard membranes if using Top Blade Roast
2. Combine Worcestershire, soy sauce, vinegar, 1/4 cup honey, and 1/2 cup bourbon. Marinate beef overnight or minimum 4 hours
3. Heat cast iron skillet to medium-high; lightly oil surface
4. Drain excess marinade from beef and discard
5. Cook steak tips 5-7 minutes until browning develops
6. Mix remaining bourbon and honey; pour over tips to glaze. Cook until caramelized to rich brown. Taste and adjust salt if needed
7. Remove from heat; cool 2-3 minutes before serving

## Prep Time: 4+ hours (marinating)
## Cook Time: 10-15 minutes
## Total Time: 30 minutes
## Servings: 6

## Nutrition (Per Serving)
403 calories | 45.1g protein
