# Tuscan Sausage Pasta

**Source:** Salt & Lavender

## Ingredients
- 8 oz uncooked pasta (fettuccine recommended)
- 11 oz Italian sausages
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup chicken broth or dry white wine
- 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tsp flour
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes
- 1 cup heavy/whipping cream
- 1 1/2 cups fresh baby spinach (loosely packed)
- Small handful fresh basil, torn or chopped
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Freshly grated parmesan cheese to taste

## Instructions
1. Boil salted water and cook pasta al dente per package directions
2. Remove sausages from casings and crumble into a skillet. Brown over medium-high heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
3. Remove sausage; discard excess fat if needed
4. Add garlic, broth, mustard, flour, and lemon juice to the pan. Stir well, scraping up browned bits. Let bubble briefly
5. Add sun-dried tomatoes and cream. Cook 2-3 minutes
6. Return sausage to pan and cook until sauce thickens
7. Stir in basil and spinach until wilted. Season with salt and pepper as needed
8. Drain pasta and toss with sauce. Serve with parmesan

## Prep Time: 10 minutes
## Cook Time: 20 minutes
## Total Time: 30 minutes
## Servings: 4
