# Wednesday: Pasta Marinara with Italian Sausage

## Ingredients
- 1 lb pasta (penne or rigatoni)
- 1 lb Italian sausage
- 24 oz marinara sauce
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- Parmesan cheese for serving
- Fresh basil for garnish

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package directions
2. In a large pan, brown the sausage and break into pieces
3. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened
4. Add marinara sauce and Italian seasoning
5. Simmer for 10 minutes
6. Toss with cooked pasta
7. Top with Parmesan and fresh basil

## Prep Time: 10 minutes
## Cook Time: 20 minutes
## Servings: 4-6
