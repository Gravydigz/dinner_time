# Marry Me Chicken Tortellini

**Source:** Flavorful Life

## Ingredients

**Chicken & Seasonings:**
- 1 lb chicken breast, cut into 1-inch chunks
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

**Base & Aromatics:**
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil, divided
- 1 shallot, minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

**Sauce & Pasta:**
- 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, finely chopped
- 1/4 cup tomato paste
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 1 lb cheese tortellini
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 3 cups spinach, roughly chopped
- 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

**Garnish:**
- Fresh basil, black pepper, red pepper flakes, parmesan

## Instructions
1. **Season chicken:** Combine chicken with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and smoked paprika in a bowl. Toss with flour until lightly coated
2. **Cook chicken:** Heat butter and 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken in an even layer; cook, tossing occasionally, until browned and cooked through. Transfer to a plate
3. **Build sauce:** Add remaining tablespoon of olive oil, garlic, shallot, and sun-dried tomatoes. Saut√© 3 minutes, stirring frequently. Season with red pepper flakes and salt/pepper. Stir in tomato paste and broth, scraping up browned bits
4. **Cook pasta:** Add tortellini; bring to a simmer. Cover and cook 3-4 minutes until tender, stirring at the 2-minute mark
5. **Finish:** Uncover and stir in cooked chicken, spinach, cream, and parmesan until combined. Serve hot with fresh basil, black pepper, and additional parmesan

## Prep Time: 10 minutes
## Cook Time: 15 minutes
## Total Time: 25 minutes
## Servings: 6
