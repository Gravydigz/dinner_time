# One Pot Skillet Ravioli Lasagna with Spinach and Kale

**Source:** Aberdeen's Kitchen

## Ingredients
- 18 oz refrigerated ravioli (9 oz wild mushroom + 9 oz cheesy parmesan)
- 14 oz can diced tomato, drained
- 14 oz tomato sauce
- 1 1/2 tsp dried basil
- 1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 3 cups baby spinach and kale, roughly chopped
- 4 oz part skim ricotta cheese
- 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
- Salt and ground black pepper to taste
- Fresh basil, roughly chopped for garnish

## Instructions
1. In a large oven-safe skillet with tall sides or dutch oven over medium-high heat, combine tomatoes, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, chicken stock, spinach, and kale with a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil
2. Reduce to a simmer and cook approximately 5 minutes until greens wilt and liquid thickens. Add salt to taste
3. Stir in ravioli and add ricotta cheese in large chunks around the pan. Return to a boil, cover, reduce heat, and simmer 7-10 minutes until ravioli cooks through
4. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Optional: broil in the oven for 2-3 minutes until cheese becomes golden and bubbly
5. Remove from heat and let rest 2-3 minutes to allow the sauce to set. Top with fresh ground black pepper and chopped basil

## Prep Time: 5 minutes
## Cook Time: 20 minutes
## Total Time: 25 minutes
## Servings: 4
